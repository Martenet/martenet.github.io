About Martenet Volatus

Martenet Volatus is an initiative targeting to develop a Swiss engineered electrical Vertical and Take Off (eVTOL) aircraft. This project will allow us to experiment and assess sustainable energy (such as fuel cell) regarding Time of flight optimisation in Urban Air Mobility (UAM).

We are exploring ways to earn revenue associated to the promotion of the brand Martenet Aerospace. This may help us to attract trustful clients. Some formats that we would like to experiment with are live streaming, podcasting, storytelling and also merchandising.
